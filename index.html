Core Speciality

CUSTOM
WIRING LOOMS

A proper custom loom is the foundation of any serious build. Not a factory loom hacked apart with scotch locks and hope — a purpose-built harness designed for the engine, the ECU, and the chassis it's actually going into.

Every loom we build is engineered from scratch: correct wire gauge for every circuit, mil-spec or Raychem DR25 sleeving, Deutsch DT/DTM connectors where they matter, and fully labelled so you or your tuner can actually work on it later.

Whether it's a full engine bay rewire, a plug-and-play ECU swap harness, or a stripped-back motorsport loom, we build it right the first time.

Not zip ties and

  • Deutsch DT / DTM / Superseal connectors
